ACME Corporation - Employee Policies and Procedures

Last Updated: January 2024

SECTION 1: WORK HOURS AND ATTENDANCE

1.1 Standard Work Hours
Full-time employees are expected to work 40 hours per week, typically from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday. However, we offer flexible working arrangements subject to manager approval.

1.2 Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week after completing their probationary period. Remote work requests must be submitted through the HR portal at least 48 hours in advance.

1.3 Attendance Tracking
All employees must clock in and out using the TimeTracker system. Tardiness of more than 15 minutes should be reported to your immediate supervisor.

SECTION 2: LEAVE POLICIES

2.1 Paid Time Off (PTO)
- New employees: 15 days per year
- Employees with 3-5 years: 20 days per year
- Employees with 5+ years: 25 days per year

PTO must be requested at least 2 weeks in advance for planned absences.

2.2 Sick Leave
Employees receive 10 days of sick leave annually. Medical certificates are required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days.

2.3 Parental Leave
- Maternity leave: 16 weeks paid leave
- Paternity leave: 8 weeks paid leave
- Adoption leave: 12 weeks paid leave

SECTION 3: COMPENSATION AND BENEFITS

3.1 Salary Review
Annual salary reviews occur in January. Performance-based increases range from 3% to 15% depending on individual and company performance.

3.2 Health Insurance
The company provides comprehensive health insurance coverage for employees and their immediate families. Coverage begins on the first day of employment.

3.3 Retirement Benefits
ACME Corporation matches 401(k) contributions up to 6% of base salary. Employees are fully vested after 3 years of service.

SECTION 4: PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT

4.1 Training Budget
Each employee has an annual training budget of $2,000 for conferences, courses, and certifications related to their role.

4.2 Tuition Reimbursement
Employees pursuing job-related degrees can receive up to $5,000 annually in tuition reimbursement. A commitment to remain with the company for 2 years post-graduation is required.

SECTION 5: CODE OF CONDUCT

5.1 Workplace Behavior
All employees must maintain professional conduct, respect diversity, and contribute to a positive work environment. Harassment of any kind will not be tolerated.

5.2 Confidentiality
Employees must protect confidential company information and client data. Non-disclosure agreements remain in effect even after employment termination.

5.3 Conflict of Interest
Employees must disclose any potential conflicts of interest to the ethics committee within 30 days of their occurrence.

For questions about these policies, contact HR at hr@acmecorp.com or extension 5000.
